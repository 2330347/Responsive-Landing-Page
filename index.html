Nestled in the vibrant Kalinga Market, Queen Café is your go-to destination for a perfect food experience. Our small and cozy space is designed to make you feel at home while you indulge in our carefully crafted menu.

From sizzling burgers to our town-famous cold coffee, every item is prepared with the freshest ingredients and a touch of love. Whether you're catching up with friends or grabbing a quick bite, Queen Café promises a smile in every bite.
